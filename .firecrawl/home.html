Why Fight Traffic Tickets?

Many drivers quickly pay their fines without realizing the snowball effect. Fighting a traffic ticket could save you far more in the long run.

Here’s how:

Insurance Increases: Convictions raise insurance rates for years. A single ticket can cost far more than the fine itself—especially if your child gets one and it hikes the whole family’s premiums.

Demerit Points: Tickets add points to your record. Too many points mean license suspensions. New drivers lose their license at 4 points, while fully licensed drivers face interviews at 8.

Licence Suspensions: Some charges like careless or distracted driving come with built-in suspensions, often hidden until it’s too late.

Employment Impact: Commercial drivers and those needing a clean record risk losing jobs or becoming uninsurable.

A ticket isn’t small—it’s a financial and career trap. Call us today for a free consultation and protect your driving record, your income, and your future.
