# Traffic Ticket Defence

Got a ticket or charge? We’ve got you covered!

### Not Every Traffic Ticket Deserves A Guilty Plea. The Right Legal Help Can Make All The Difference.

A traffic ticket might seem like a quick inconvenience, but the consequences can follow you for years—demerit points, higher insurance premiums, costly fines, and a permanent mark on your driving record.

That’s why working with a traffic ticket infraction lawyer who specializes in defending these charges is critical. We focus exclusively on traffic ticket defence across Toronto and the GTA, using proven strategies to challenge evidence, identify procedural errors, and build strong cases for our clients.

Why should you fight your ticket?

You’ll avoid demerit points, protect your insurance rates, reduce or eliminate fines, and keep your record clean.

Many infractions—such as speeding, disobeying traffic signs, unsafe turns, and HOV violations—are often based on subjective observations or incomplete evidence, making them highly contestable with the right approach.

With a 98% win rate, our team has the experience and track record to deliver results. Traffic ticket infractions aren’t just part of what we do—they’re our specialization. Don’t accept penalties that could impact your future.
