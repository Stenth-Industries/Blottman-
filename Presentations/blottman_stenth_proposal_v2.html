05 — Stenth's Strategy
The Game Plan

HOW STENTH
FIXES & GROWS THIS

Phase 1 — Week 1
Fix the Foundation
  • Install proper conversion tracking (calls + forms)
  • Connect Google Analytics & Tag Manager
  • Audit and fix all policy violations
  • Pause all zero-performing campaigns
  • Enable proven "IGNORE" campaigns immediately
  • Clean up account structure & naming
  • Set up call tracking so every lead is counted
Phase 2 — Week 2–3
Launch Smart Google Ads
  • Separate campaigns per ticket type (Stunt, Speeding, Distracted, DUI)
  • Phrase & exact match only — no wasted broad match spend
  • Add 50+ negative keywords from day one
  • Tight Ontario geo-targeting by city & region
  • Urgency-driven ad copy ("15-Day Deadline? Call Now")
  • Call extensions & call-only ads for mobile users
Phase 3 — Month 2
Expand to Meta & Social
  • Launch Facebook & Instagram awareness + retargeting ads
  • TikTok ads targeting Ontario drivers (massive reach, low cost)
  • Retarget website visitors who didn't call or submit a form
  • Video ads: "Got a ticket? Here's what to do in 60 seconds"
  • Budget split: 60% Google · 25% Meta · 15% TikTok/Other
  • Build landing pages per ticket type for higher conversion rates
Phase 4 — Ongoing
Optimize, Report & Scale
  • Weekly search terms review — continuously add negatives
  • A/B test ad headlines, CTAs, and landing pages
  • Switch to Target CPA bidding once enough data builds
  • Monthly performance report with cost per lead breakdown
  • Scale budget into highest-converting campaigns
  • Seasonal strategy (summer = more tickets on the road)
