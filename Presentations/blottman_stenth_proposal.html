Lead Model

Clients need a lawyer urgently after getting a ticket. The window to act is short — typically 15 days to respond. Calls and form submissions are the primary lead types.

Target Customer
Any Ontario Driver

Anyone who just received a traffic ticket in Ontario. High urgency — they're searching for help within hours of getting the ticket. High intent, short decision window.

Fixed fee per ticket type. High-value cases (stunt driving, DUI, careless driving) can be worth $800–$2,500+ per client. Even a few leads per month justifies the ad spend.

04 — Stenth's Strategy
Game Plan

HOW STENTH
FIXES THIS

Phase 1 — Week 1
Fix the Foundation
  • Install proper conversion tracking (calls + form fills)
  • Connect Google Analytics & Tag Manager
  • Audit and resolve policy violations
  • Pause all zero-performing campaigns
  • Enable "IGNORE" & "IGNOREE" campaigns (proven converters)
  • Clean up account naming & structure
Phase 2 — Week 2–3
Launch Smart Campaigns
  • Build high-intent keyword campaigns by ticket type
  • Focus on phrase & exact match keywords only
  • Separate campaigns: Speeding / Stunt / DUI / Distracted
  • Tight Ontario geo-targeting by region
  • Write urgency-driven ad copy ("Get Help Today")
  • Set up call extensions & call-only ads
Phase 3 — Month 2
Optimize & Scale
  • Weekly review of search terms — add negatives
  • A/B test ad headlines & CTAs
  • Switch to Target CPA bidding once data builds
  • Scale budget into what's converting
  • Build landing pages per ticket type
  • Review Quality Score and improve ad relevance
Phase 4 — Ongoing
Grow & Report
  • Monthly performance reports to client
  • Track cost per lead monthly
  • Add new practice areas as they grow
  • Explore Performance Max for awareness
  • Leverage reviews in ads (star ratings)
  • Seasonal adjustments (summer = more tickets)
